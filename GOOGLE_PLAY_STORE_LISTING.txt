TrimFit is your all-in-one smart fitness coach — designed to help you lose weight, build muscle, stay fit, and track every step of your fitness journey. Whether you're a complete beginner or a seasoned athlete, TrimFit adapts to your goals and provides personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and detailed progress tracking.

🎯 WHY TRIMFIT?

• 205+ PROFESSIONAL EXERCISES — Browse our massive library of exercises across 7 categories: Abs, Back, Chest, Legs, Shoulders, Arms, and Cardio. Each exercise includes step-by-step instructions, target muscle details, difficulty levels, and video demonstrations.

• SMART WORKOUT GENERATION — Tell TrimFit your goals, fitness level, and available equipment, and it generates a personalized workout plan just for you. No more guessing what to do at the gym.

• PERSONALIZED NUTRITION TRACKING — Log your meals, track calories and macros, monitor water intake, and get smart meal plan recommendations tailored to your goals. Whether you want to lose weight, bulk up, or eat cleaner, TrimFit helps you stay on track.

• DETAILED PROGRESS ANALYTICS — Track body measurements, log before & after photos, monitor workout streaks, and visualize your progress with beautiful charts and reports. Celebrate your achievements with our badge and milestone system.

• WORKOUT STREAKS & ACHIEVEMENTS — Stay motivated with daily streak tracking, unlock achievement badges for hitting milestones, and compete in community fitness challenges.
